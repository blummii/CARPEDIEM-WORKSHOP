KICH BAN CA SU DUNG QUAN LY BAN HANG

1. Quan ly san pham

Ten ca su dung Quan ly san pham
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly xem danh sach san pham, tim kiem san pham va thuc hien cac thao tac quan ly bao gom them moi san pham, sua thong tin san pham va xoa san pham khong con can thiet. He thong dong thoi cho phep xem danh sach san pham theo phan trang.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap chuc nang "Quan ly san pham".
  • He thong hien thi giao dien quan ly san pham gom cac nhom chuc nang:
    • Them san pham moi
    • Tim san pham hien co
    • Sua san pham hien co
    • Xoa san pham
    • Xem toan bo san pham
    • Phan trang danh sach san pham

Truong hop 1: Them san pham moi

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Them san pham moi".
  • He thong hien thi form tao san pham moi.
  • Tac nhan nhap: danh muc, ten san pham, gia, mo ta, hinh anh (neu co), so luong ton ban dau, muc ton toi thieu.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong tao ma san pham, luu thong tin san pham va khoi tao du lieu ton kho thanh cong.

Truong hop 2: Tim san pham hien co

  • Tac nhan nhap thong tin san pham can tim vao thanh tim kiem.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Tim".
  • He thong loc theo ten san pham, ma san pham, mo ta hoac ten danh muc.
  • He thong tra ve ket qua phu hop.

Truong hop 3: Sua san pham

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Sua".
  • He thong hien thi thong tin san pham hien tai.
  • Tac nhan cap nhat danh muc, ten san pham, gia, mo ta hoac hinh anh.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong cap nhat san pham thanh cong.

Truong hop 4: Xoa san pham

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xoa".
  • He thong yeu cau xac nhan thao tac.
  • Tac nhan xac nhan xoa.
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu lien quan. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 2.
  • He thong xoa san pham khoi he thong.

Truong hop 5: Xem danh sach san pham

  • He thong hien thi toan bo danh sach san pham hien co.
  • Neu so luong san pham lon, he thong chia danh sach thanh nhieu trang, moi trang hien thi 10 san pham.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu thong tin nhap khong hop le, thieu sot hoac tep hinh anh khong dung dinh dang, he thong thong bao loi cu the cho nguoi tac nhan biet.
  • Luong phu 2: Neu san pham dang co du lieu lien quan va khong the xoa, he thong thong bao loi cho tac nhan.
  • Luong phu 3: Neu khong tim thay san pham phu hop, he thong thong bao khong co ket qua.
Ket qua tra ve Qua trinh quan ly san pham dien ra thanh cong; thong tin san pham duoc luu, cap nhat hoac xoa dung theo thao tac; danh sach san pham duoc hien thi day du theo ket qua tim kiem va phan trang tren he thong.

2. Quan ly khach hang

Ten ca su dung Quan ly khach hang
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly xem danh sach khach hang, tim kiem khach hang, them moi khach hang, sua thong tin khach hang, xoa khach hang va xem lich su mua hang cua tung khach hang. He thong dong thoi ho tro phan trang danh sach khach hang.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap chuc nang "Quan ly khach hang".
  • He thong hien thi giao dien quan ly khach hang gom cac nhom chuc nang:
    • Them khach hang moi
    • Tim khach hang hien co
    • Sua khach hang hien co
    • Xoa khach hang
    • Xem lich su mua hang
    • Xem toan bo khach hang
    • Phan trang danh sach khach hang

Truong hop 1: Them khach hang moi

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Them khach hang moi".
  • He thong hien thi form tao khach hang moi.
  • Tac nhan nhap: ten khach hang, so dien thoai, email.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Them".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong va kiem tra trung so dien thoai. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong tao ma khach hang va them moi khach hang thanh cong.

Truong hop 2: Tim khach hang hien co

  • Tac nhan nhap ten khach hang hoac so dien thoai can tim vao thanh tim kiem.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Tim".
  • He thong loc theo ten khach hang hoac so dien thoai.
  • He thong tra ve ket qua phu hop.

Truong hop 3: Sua khach hang

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Sua".
  • He thong hien thi thong tin khach hang hien tai.
  • Tac nhan cap nhat ten khach hang, so dien thoai hoac email.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong cap nhat khach hang thanh cong.

Truong hop 4: Xoa khach hang

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xoa".
  • He thong yeu cau xac nhan thao tac.
  • Tac nhan xac nhan xoa.
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 2.
  • He thong xoa khach hang khoi he thong.

Truong hop 5: Xem lich su mua hang

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xem lich su mua hang" tai khach hang can xem.
  • He thong hien thi danh sach don hang da mua cua khach hang gom ma don, thoi gian, so luong san pham, tong tien va trang thai don hang.

Truong hop 6: Xem danh sach khach hang

  • He thong hien thi toan bo danh sach khach hang hien co.
  • Neu so luong khach hang lon, he thong chia danh sach thanh nhieu trang, moi trang hien thi 10 khach hang.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu thong tin nhap khong hop le, so dien thoai khong dung dinh dang hoac da ton tai trong he thong, he thong thong bao loi cu the cho nguoi tac nhan biet.
  • Luong phu 2: Neu khach hang khong the xoa do du lieu lien quan, he thong thong bao loi cho tac nhan.
  • Luong phu 3: Neu khong tim thay khach hang phu hop, he thong thong bao khong co ket qua.
Ket qua tra ve Qua trinh quan ly khach hang dien ra thanh cong; thong tin khach hang duoc luu, cap nhat hoac xoa dung theo thao tac; lich su mua hang duoc hien thi day du; danh sach khach hang duoc hien thi theo ket qua tim kiem va phan trang tren he thong.

3. Quan ly don hang

Ten ca su dung Quan ly don hang
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly xem danh sach don hang, tim kiem va loc don hang, tao don hang moi, xem chi tiet don hang, cap nhat trang thai don hang va xoa don hang. He thong dong thoi ho tro phan trang danh sach don hang va dong bo ton kho theo trang thai don.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap chuc nang "Quan ly don hang".
  • He thong hien thi giao dien quan ly don hang gom cac nhom chuc nang:
    • Tim kiem va loc don hang
    • Tao don hang moi
    • Xem chi tiet don hang
    • Cap nhat trang thai don hang
    • Xoa don hang
    • Xem toan bo don hang
    • Phan trang danh sach don hang

Truong hop 1: Tim kiem va loc don hang

  • Tac nhan nhap ma don hang, ten khach hang hoac so dien thoai vao o tim kiem.
  • Tac nhan co the nhap them ngay, thang, nam de loc.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Loc".
  • He thong thuc hien tim kiem va loc theo cac dieu kien da nhap.
  • He thong tra ve ket qua phu hop.

Truong hop 2: Tao don hang moi

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Tao don hang moi".
  • He thong hien thi form tao don hang.
  • Tac nhan chon khach hang.
  • Tac nhan chon san pham, so luong cua tung san pham trong don.
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong va kiem tra so luong san pham khong vuot qua ton kho kha dung. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu don".
  • He thong tao ma don hang, luu don hang va chi tiet don hang thanh cong.

Truong hop 3: Xem chi tiet don hang

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xem chi tiet".
  • He thong hien thi thong tin don hang hien tai, bao gom thong tin khach hang, danh sach san pham, so luong, don gia, thanh tien va trang thai don hang.

Truong hop 4: Cap nhat trang thai don hang

  • Tac nhan chon trang thai moi cho don hang.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu ton kho. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 2.
  • He thong cap nhat trang thai don hang.
  • Nếu don hang chuyen sang nhom trang thai can tru kho, he thong tu dong tru ton kho.
  • Neu don hang chuyen tu nhom da tru kho sang nhom khong tru kho, he thong tu dong hoan lai ton kho.

Truong hop 5: Xoa don hang

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xoa".
  • He thong yeu cau xac nhan thao tac.
  • Tac nhan xac nhan xoa.
  • He thong xoa chi tiet don hang va don hang khoi he thong.

Truong hop 6: Xem danh sach don hang

  • He thong hien thi toan bo danh sach don hang hien co.
  • Neu so luong don hang lon, he thong chia danh sach thanh nhieu trang, moi trang hien thi 10 don hang.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu thieu khach hang, don hang khong co san pham hoac so luong san pham vuot qua ton kho kha dung, he thong thong bao loi cu the cho nguoi tac nhan biet.
  • Luong phu 2: Neu khong du ton kho de cap nhat trang thai don hang, he thong tu choi cap nhat va thong bao loi cho tac nhan.
  • Luong phu 3: Neu khong tim thay don hang phu hop, he thong thong bao khong co ket qua.
Ket qua tra ve Qua trinh quan ly don hang dien ra thanh cong; don hang duoc tao moi, tim kiem, loc, cap nhat trang thai hoac xoa dung theo thao tac; thong tin ton kho duoc dong bo phu hop voi trang thai don; danh sach don hang duoc hien thi day du theo ket qua tim kiem va phan trang tren he thong.

4. Quan ly ton kho

Ten ca su dung Quan ly ton kho
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly theo doi danh sach ton kho, tim kiem san pham trong kho, cap nhat so luong ton va muc ton toi thieu, dong thoi xem trang thai con hang hoac het hang cua tung san pham. He thong ho tro phan trang danh sach ton kho.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap chuc nang "Quan ly ton kho".
  • He thong hien thi giao dien quan ly ton kho gom cac nhom chuc nang:
    • Tim san pham ton kho
    • Xem so luong ton kho
    • Xem muc ton toi thieu
    • Sua ton kho
    • Xem trang thai con hang hoac het hang
    • Phan trang danh sach ton kho

Truong hop 1: Tim san pham ton kho

  • Tac nhan nhap ten san pham hoac ma san pham vao thanh tim kiem.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Tim".
  • He thong loc theo ten san pham hoac ma san pham.
  • He thong tra ve ket qua phu hop.

Truong hop 2: Cap nhat ton kho

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Sua".
  • He thong hien thi thong tin ton kho hien tai.
  • Tac nhan cap nhat so luong ton kho va muc ton toi thieu.
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong cap nhat ton kho thanh cong.

Truong hop 3: Xem danh sach ton kho

  • He thong hien thi toan bo danh sach ton kho hien co.
  • He thong hien thi trang thai con hang hoac het hang dua tren so luong ton va muc ton toi thieu.
  • Neu so luong san pham ton kho lon, he thong chia danh sach thanh nhieu trang, moi trang hien thi 10 san pham.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu thong tin nhap khong hop le hoac thieu sot, he thong thong bao loi cu the cho nguoi tac nhan biet.
  • Luong phu 2: Neu khong tim thay san pham ton kho phu hop, he thong thong bao khong co ket qua.
Ket qua tra ve Qua trinh quan ly ton kho dien ra thanh cong; du lieu ton kho duoc cap nhat dung theo thao tac; danh sach ton kho va trang thai ton kho duoc hien thi day du theo ket qua tim kiem va phan trang tren he thong.

5. Dashboard ban hang

Ten ca su dung Xem dashboard ban hang
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly xem tong quan tinh hinh ban hang tren he thong thong qua cac chi so thong ke nhu so luong don hang, khach hang, san pham va tinh trang van hanh de phuc vu theo doi va ra quyet dinh.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap trang dashboard.
  • He thong tong hop du lieu tu cac phan he quan ly ban hang.
  • He thong hien thi cac thong tin tong quan ve tinh hinh hoat dong tren giao dien dashboard.
  • Tac nhan theo doi cac chi so de quan sat tinh hinh ban hang va quan ly van hanh.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu du lieu thong ke tam thoi khong kha dung, he thong hien thi gia tri mac dinh hoac thong bao phu hop.
Ket qua tra ve Quan ly xem duoc thong tin tong quan ve tinh hinh ban hang tren he thong, ho tro theo doi hoat dong va dua ra quyet dinh quan ly phu hop.

6. Quan ly tai khoan (Admin)

Ten ca su dung Quan ly tai khoan (Admin)
Mo ta Chuc nang nay cho phep nguoi quan ly quan tri he thong xem danh sach tai khoan admin, them tai khoan moi, sua thong tin tai khoan (username, quyen neu co) va doi mat khau, dong thoi xoa tai khoan khong con su dung. He thong khong cho phep xoa chinh tai khoan dang dang nhap va phai giu it nhat mot tai khoan admin trong he thong.
Tac nhan Quan ly cua hang (Admin)
Dieu kien dau vao Quan ly da dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem va co quyen truy cap trang quan ly tai khoan.
Luong su kien
Luong su kien chinh
  • Quan ly dang nhap thanh cong vao he thong quan tri cua Carpe Diem.
  • Quan ly truy cap chuc nang "Quan ly tai khoan".
  • He thong hien thi giao dien quan ly tai khoan gom cac nhom chuc nang:
    • Them tai khoan admin moi
    • Sua tai khoan admin
    • Doi mat khau tai khoan (neu he thong co cot mat khau trong CSDL)
    • Xoa tai khoan admin
    • Xem danh sach tai khoan admin

Truong hop 1: Them tai khoan admin moi

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Them tai khoan".
  • He thong hien thi form them tai khoan.
  • Tac nhan nhap: username, mat khau (va quyen neu co).
  • Tac nhan nhan "Them tai khoan".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu he thong va kiem tra trung username. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong ma hoa mat khau va luu tai khoan admin moi thanh cong.

Truong hop 2: Sua tai khoan admin

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Sua" tren tai khoan can chinh sua.
  • He thong hien thi form sua thong tin tai khoan.
  • Tac nhan cap nhat username, quyen (neu co) va/hoac mat khau moi (co the de trong neu khong doi).
  • Tac nhan nhan "Luu".
  • He thong kiem tra du lieu. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 1.
  • He thong cap nhat tai khoan thanh cong.

Truong hop 3: Xoa tai khoan admin

  • Tac nhan chon thao tac "Xoa" tren tai khoan can xoa.
  • He thong yeu cau xac nhan thao tac.
  • Tac nhan xac nhan xoa.
  • He thong kiem tra rang buoc: khong duoc xoa chinh minh va phai con it nhat mot tai khoan admin. Neu khong hop le thuc hien luong phu 2.
  • He thong xoa tai khoan admin khoi he thong.

Truong hop 4: Xem danh sach tai khoan admin

  • He thong hien thi danh sach tai khoan admin hien co.
Luong su kien phu
  • Luong phu 1: Neu thong tin nhap khong hop le (thieu username/mat khau), username da ton tai, hoac he thong khong co cot mat khau trong CSDL, he thong thong bao loi cu the cho nguoi tac nhan biet.
  • Luong phu 2: Neu tac nhan co gang xoa chinh tai khoan dang dang nhap hoac he thong chi con 1 tai khoan admin, he thong tu choi va thong bao ly do.
Ket qua tra ve Qua trinh quan ly tai khoan dien ra thanh cong; tai khoan admin duoc them moi, cap nhat hoac xoa dung theo thao tac; he thong dam bao an toan bang viec khong cho phep xoa chinh minh va luon giu it nhat mot tai khoan admin.
